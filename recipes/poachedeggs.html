Poached Eggs

Ingredients (unordered)

  • 4 organic eggs
  • 2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch dried dill, or to taste

Steps (ordered)

    Step: 1

  1. Fill a large saucepan with 2 to 3 inches of water and bring to a simmer.
    2. Reduce the heat to medium-low, pour in vinegar, and the water at a gentle simmer.

    Step: 2

  2. Crack an egg into a small cup. Place cup near the surface of the hot water and gently drop egg into the water.
    3. Repeat with remaining eggs. Turn off heat, cover, and let sit until whites are set, 4 minutes. Lift eggs out of pan with a slotted spoon. Season with salt, pepper, and dill.
